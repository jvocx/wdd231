JVOCX Travel - Site Plan

Site Name

JVOCX Travel

This name represents a personal brand focused on motorcycle travel, adventure, and storytelling.

Site Purpose

This website will document a motorcycle journey from Fortaleza to Argentina, sharing travel experiences, routes, tips, and storytelling content. It will serve as both an inspirational platform and a practical guide for travelers.

Scenarios

  • How can I travel long distances on a motorcycle with a low budget?
  • What routes and stops are recommended from Brazil to Argentina?

Color Scheme

  • #0B3D91 (Blue): Used for headers, navigation, and titles.
  • #F4A261 (Orange): Used for buttons, highlights, and accents.

Typography

Montserrat: Used for headings and titles.

Roboto: Used for body text and readability.
